# Legal Red Flags & Predatory Clauses

This reference identifies common clauses in consumer contracts, terms of service, and privacy policies that are often problematic or unfair.

## 🔴 High Risk (Severe Impact)

### 1. Forced Arbitration & Class Action Waiver
*   **What it is:** A clause that prevents you from suing the company in a public court. Instead, disputes must be settled in private arbitration.
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Arbitration favors the company (which pays for the arbitrator) and lacks a public record or easy appeal process. Class action waivers prevent users from joining together for common grievances.
*   **Keywords:** "Arbitration", "Exclusive Dispute Resolution", "Waiver of Jury Trial", "Class Action Waiver".

### 2. Broad Indemnification
*   **What it is:** A requirement that you pay for the company’s legal fees or damages if they get sued because of something you did (or sometimes even if it wasn't your fault).
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Can lead to massive financial liability for the consumer.
*   **Keywords:** "Indemnify", "Hold Harmless", "Assume Liability".

### 3. Automatic Renewal (Negative Option)
*   **What it is:** The contract automatically renews unless you cancel within a very narrow window.
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Designed to trap users into paying for services they no longer use.
*   **Keywords:** "Automatically Renew", "Negative Option", "Notice Window".

### 4. Unilateral Modification
*   **What it is:** The company can change the terms of the agreement at any time, often without notice.
*   **Why it's a red flag:** You might agree to one deal today, but the company can change the "fine print" tomorrow without your consent.
*   **Keywords:** "Reserve the Right to Modify", "At our Sole Discretion", "Without Prior Notice".

## 🟡 Medium Risk (Potential Issues)

### 1. Broad Data Monetization
*   **What it is:** Use of your personal data for marketing or selling to "unspecified partners".
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Privacy loss and potential for targeted profiling.
*   **Keywords:** "Third Party Partners", "Marketing Purposes", "Affiliates", "Non-Personal Data Sharing".

### 2. High Termination Fees / Early Cancellation Penalties
*   **What it is:** Significant costs for ending a service early.
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Acts as a barrier to competition and locks consumers in.
*   **Keywords:** "Cancellation Fee", "Restocking Fee", "Liquidated Damages".

### 3. Jurisdiction / Choice of Law
*   **What it is:** Forcing legal disputes to happen in a distant state or country.
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Makes it practically impossible for you to defend your rights.
*   **Keywords:** "Governing Law", "Jurisdiction of [State/Country]".

---

## 🟢 Low Risk (General Transparency)

### 1. Vague Contact Information
*   **What it is:** Lack of a clear physical address or phone number.
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Harder to serve legal notice or get support.
*   **Keywords:** "Contact Us form only".

### 2. Overly Complex Legalese
*   **What it is:** Using 50 words when 5 would do.
*   **Why it's a red flag:** Discourages users from reading and understanding the terms.
